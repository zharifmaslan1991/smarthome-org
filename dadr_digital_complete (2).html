Golden Rules — Every Weekly Session
Come with the actual number, not an estimate. If you don't know it, find it before the meeting.
1.5 hours maximum. Tight agenda, structured. Off-topic items go to a separate conversation or email.
Every session ends with at least one action item — named owner, clear deadline. No open loops.
Bring your top blocker. What is the one thing stopping you from hitting target this week?
Green means on track. Red means you need help. Be honest — hiding a red makes it worse.
Weekly check-ins feed the monthly report. Track everything discussed — it becomes your data.
